Sweet and Spicy Gochujang Chicken

Description

If you haven't heard of this by now you're probably alergic to something

Ingredients

  • Gochujang
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Honey
  • Garlic
  • Sesame oil
  • Ginger
  • Canola oil
  • Chicken
  • Green Onion
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Make the sauce in a bowl
  2. Cook the chicken in a wok.
  3. Add the sauce to the wok (with the chicken) and cook until thick and bubbly.
  4. Garnish and serve over rice
