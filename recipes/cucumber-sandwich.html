Cumcumber Sandwich

Description

This sandwich is very cool.

It saved 3 babies from a burning building and won the best sandwich award for excellence

Ingredients

  • 2 thick slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 tablespoons cream cheese, softened
  • 6 slices cucumber
  • 2 tablespoons alfafa sprouts
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red wine vinegar
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 leaf lettuce, leafy
  • 1 ounce pepperoncini, sliced
  • 1/2 avocado, mashed

Steps

  1. Spread each slice of bread with 1 tablespoon cream cheese.
  2. On one slice of bread, arrange cucumber slices in a single layer.
  3. Cover with sprouts
  4. Sprinkle with oil and vinegar
  5. Layer tomato slices, lettuce, and pepperoncini.
  6. Spread other slice of bread with mashed avocado.
  7. Close sandwich and serve immediately.
